About Me

Hi, I am Sai Bankar, a Mechatronics Engineering graduate and a technology enthusiast. My expertise spans automation, PLC programming, and robotics, empowering me to create smarter systems that solve real-world challenges. I am passionate about merging innovation with creative design to develop solutions that push the boundaries of technology.

Over the years, I have worked on diverse projects, from developing AI-powered virtual influencers to crafting immersive AR/VR applications. These projects reflect my commitment to integrating cutting-edge technologies with user-centered design to redefine digital interaction.

My journey has led me to explore areas such as photogrammetry, 3D scanning, and haptic integration with AR/VR systems. I firmly believe in creating seamless, intuitive interfaces that bridge the gap between the physical and digital worlds.

I am always eager to collaborate on groundbreaking projects that expand the horizon of what technology can achieve.

Projects

Wearable Animal Distraction Device - 'Rakshak'

'Rakshak' is a wearable device designed to deter wild animals from approaching human settlements. It uses ultrasonic signals and visual stimuli to keep animals at bay. This project aims to enhance safety for rural communities while preserving wildlife.

WebAR Virtual Shoe Try-On

This project allows users to virtually try on various shoe models using WebAR technology. Users can select from thumbnail images of different shoes, and see them displayed on their feet through their device's camera. The application includes hand tracking for precise fitting.

MedIQ - AI Medical Chatbot

MedIQ is an AI-powered chatbot designed to explain complex medical terms and concepts in simple language. It provides users with instant, reliable information, making it an ideal tool for patients, caregivers, and anyone seeking a better understanding of medical topics.

Brain Impact Simulator - Smart Helmet

This project involves the development of a smart helmet equipped with sensors to detect the point of impact during an injury. The Brain Impact Simulator provides real-time data on the location and severity of the impact, offering valuable insights for safety and medical evaluation.
